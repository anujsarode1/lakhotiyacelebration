About Our Hall

Creating memorable moments since years

Lakhotiya Celebration Hall is a premium event venue located in the heart of Nagpur, India. We specialize in hosting weddings, engagements, birthdays, corporate events, and all types of celebrations. Our spacious venue combines elegance with comfort, ensuring your special day becomes a cherished memory.

With a reputation for excellence and hospitality, we strive to make every event a grand success. Our dedicated team works closely with you to understand your requirements and deliver a seamless experience.
